Post-Tw.In.Yo.Go.Fa.


Presented as part of Luminaria Contemporary Arts Festival, Post-Tw.In.Yo.Go.Fa., is the name of the dystopian digital realm that imagines life after the fall of five internet beasts (Twitter, Instagram, Youtube, Google, and Facebook). By using the hashtag #luminariasa people engaged with the artwork and were able to see their posts altered and disrupted on the screen in real time.
