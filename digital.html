Post-Data


Post-Data is an immersive audiovisual installation between the physical and virtual world that allows willing participants to "insert themselves" by creating Instagram stories on their public accounts with a specific hashtag, for it to be fed into a Java written software that generates screenshots which are then exploded into fragmented patches of color, sorted in a grid on the left side of the screen and displayed in a more fluid random fashion on the right half with generated audio. This piece is inspired by the yearn to creatively use the limitations of social media, in this case being Instagram, to create interventions and alternate pop-up social spaces.
