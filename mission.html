Our mission is to evaluate our digital spaces by considering its architecture and its material, how we move around them, how free we are in them, and how we consume and create inside them. When we began our collaboration, we shared an enthusiasm for open source software and DIY equipment to create generative and algorithmic art. It later expanded into a restless attempt of trying to make sense of our social medias and trying to stretch its functionality and possibility for creative expression outside of its intended use. Day to day features such as avatars and user interfaces became a possible medium to create with, not solely working and manipulating its apeparance, but also its functionality. We are not hackers, but we do code to create and find our limits.
